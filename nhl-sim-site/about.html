About the NHL What-If Simulator

This tool lets you pick any 32 NHL squads (across eras), simulate an 82-game season, view standings & playoff brackets. Built in Streamlit, embedded in this static site.

How It Works

  • Select your 32 teams and seasons
  • Run a full-season sim or a one-game sim
  • View standings, playoffs, and series details
